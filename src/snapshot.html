qr show is an exhibition hosted at the recurse center. it features many, any, & all things "qr code" including:
  • protocols: technical deep dives & exploits
  • history: predecessors, competitors, & lore
  • payloads: data inside qr codes
  • art: aesthetic indulgence
what kinds of submissions are we accepting? all kinds! (besides inappropriate ones) for example:
  • qr codes that cause hiccups & incite violence laughter
  • collections of qr codes found in the wild
  • devices that generate / consume qr codes
  • qr codes made out of ...? moss?
does your submission have to be your own work?
