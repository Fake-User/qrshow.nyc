qr show was an exhibition organized by greg sadetsky & sasha friedenberg featuring many, any, & all things "qr code". it was hosted by the recurse center on sunday march 16th 2025 in brooklyn nyc. this is a directory of the authors & an archive of their exhibits.

this archive is still in development & not comprehensive. if you presented something that hasnt been listed & would like to share it, please email self@destruct.dev

to everyone who attended - thank you for coming. it was wonderful to have you all.

to the volunteers - thank you for all your help setting up, cleaning up, & being very cool.

to the authors - thank you for your sharing your spectaculare creations with all of us.

to the recurse center - thank you for being the most generous hosts. you are incredible people!
